## 👩‍💻 About Me

> *"I work at the intersection of compliance engineering and public safety technology  replacing manual checkbox processes with repeatable, auditor-ready outputs."*

I'm a **GRC and Identity Engineer** with experience across two regulated environments: **Identity Governance and Administration (IGA)** in financial services, and **hands-on technical work in a FedRAMP High environment** serving federal and state/local law enforcement agencies.

My IGA background covers privileged access monitoring, user access reviews, and RBAC analysis — work that demands precision in regulated settings where access decisions carry real accountability.

On the compliance engineering side, I build **AWS-native tools** that automate audit evidence collection and compliance workflows — turning manual, error-prone processes into repeatable, scriptable, auditor-ready outputs mapped directly to **NIST 800-53 Rev 5**, **FedRAMP High**, and **CJIS Security Policy v6.0**.

| FedRAMP High · CJIS · Federal & SLED | Privileged Access · RBAC · Access Reviews | AWS-native · Auditor-ready · Scriptable |

## 🎯 What I'm Building

> A connected suite of open-source GRC automation tools  each one a piece of a larger compliance pipeline.

```
Audit Tools detect  →  Config Monitor watches  →  Remediation fixes
       →  Evidence Logger collects  →  Compliance Report visualizes
```

My work sits at the intersection of three disciplines:

- **GRC Engineering** — automating audit evidence collection and compliance workflows, mapping tools to CJIS Security Policy, FedRAMP, and NIST 800-53 controls
- **Identity Engineering (IAM/IGA)** — streamlining access reviews and provisioning pipelines, applying AC-family control requirements to real infrastructure
- **Cloud Security** — building AWS security tooling aligned to compliance baselines
